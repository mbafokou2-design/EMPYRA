About Us

Who Are We?

EMPYRA MINING INNOVATIONS (EMI) is a Cameroonian company operating in the mining sector. EMI relies on innovation and mining intelligence to help establish sustainable practices that will positively impact mining in Cameroon.

Our vision is to contribute to the creation of sustainable and innovative operating conditions for the mining sector, in line with the environmental, social and economic aspirations of the Cameroonian State.
