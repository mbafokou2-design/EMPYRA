Mining Operations Assistance

Home Services Mining Operations Assistance

Mining Operation Assistance is a key process for improving efficiency, safety, and cost-effectiveness in mining, while taking into account environmental and social constraints.

Our Approach: Tap into our experience of working on various projects. Through a combination of systematic process analysis, a network of discipline specialists, and a clear understanding of practical improvements, we identify opportunities and help you bring them to reality.

Operational Expertise: Our operational support teams are led by experienced consultants with strong mine site expertise. We work closely with clients to identify root causes of operational issues and determine whether solutions lie in improved analysis, clear performance indicators, new technologies, or process re-engineering.

Integrated Solutions: We assemble multidisciplinary teams within our company to deliver the technical, operational, and linguistic capabilities required to successfully implement solutions.

Environmental & Social Management: Proactive environmental planning allows you to minimize risks while addressing key environmental and social challenges. Our experts support compliance with regulations and deliver cost-effective and technically sound solutions.

From initial resource mapping to mine closure and rehabilitation, we conduct environmental and social impact assessments in accordance with international standards.

Our Services

  • Grade control and geometallurgical analysis
  • Data analytics of mining efficiencies
  • Environmental and social impact assessments
  • Mine-to-mill optimization
  • Simulation of complex mining environments to improve efficiency
  • Training
  • Backfill management
  • Decarbonization strategies (including power savings and waste management)
  • Closure of mining sites
  • Rehabilitation of abandoned mining sites
